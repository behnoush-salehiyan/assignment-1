Terms and Conditions for Placing Orders

  1. Correct and Complete Information:
    The customer is responsible for providing correct and complete contact details and address. Any errors resulting from incorrect information entered are the customer's responsibility.
  2. Payment:
    Payment methods include online payment and cash on delivery. Orders with online payment will be prioritized for processing.
  3. Order Cancellation:
    The customer may cancel their order only within 2 hours after placing it. After this period, the order will be prepared for dispatch.
  4. Delivery Time:
    The delivery time for orders is between 3 to 5 working days. Customized products may require additional time for delivery.
  5. Customized Products:
    For products designed or manufactured based on the customer's specific request, cancellations or refunds are not allowed.
  6. Product Returns:
    If there is a defect or issue with the product, the customer must contact the support team within 24 hours of receiving the order.
  7. Shipping Costs:
    Shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer and will be calculated at the time of order placement. Free shipping is offered for orders above 1 million Toman.
  8. Order Tracking:
    A tracking code will be sent to the customer's contact number or email after the order is placed. Customers can check the status of their order via the "Track Order" section.
  9. Limited Products:
    Some products may have limited availability due to material constraints or quantity limits.
  10. Order Modification:
    If the customer needs to change the details of their order (such as address, quantity, etc.), they must contact the support team within 1 hour of placing the order.
